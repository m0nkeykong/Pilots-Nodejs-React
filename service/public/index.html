    PILOTS - RESTful Service API Documentation

          General Description

      This is a general-purpose platform for creators to help bring creative projects to life with the help of consumers who decide which project they are willing to contribute credits to.
      As a result, help creators to reach the goal they set for themselves and helping creators to publish their projects.
      This model tracks its roots to subscription model of arts patronage, where artists would go directly to their audiences to fund their work
